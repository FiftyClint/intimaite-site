Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?
Yes, absolutely. All your data is stored locally on your device by default. We offer optional biometric and passcode protection, and you can enable or disable any cloud backups. Your intimate data never leaves your control.
Can I use this app with my partner?
Yes! intimaite is designed for both solo users and partners. You can share a device and track collaboratively, or maintain separate accounts and share insights as you prefer.
What if I want to track something not included in the app?
We've built intimaite to be highly customizable. You can add custom positions, toys, or other details that matter to you. The app grows with your needs and preferences.
Is there a subscription fee?
intimaite offers both free and premium tiers. The core tracking features are available for free, while advanced analytics and additional customization are available via a modest subscription that helps us maintain the service without selling your data.
How do I export or delete my data?
You have complete control over your data. You can export it to JSON format at any time from the Settings screen. You can also delete individual entries or clear all data with confirmation steps to prevent accidental deletion.
